MoodWave

Mood Wave is an innovative music playlist generator that uses the Spotify API to curate personalized playlists based on your mood. By connecting to your Spotify account, the app prompts you to select your current mood, then leverages mood-specific music recommendations to create a tailored listening experience. Designed during a hackathon, Mood Wave combines music discovery with emotional expression, offering the perfect soundtrack for every moment.
